About EcoSnap

EcoSnap empowers users to make environmentally conscious decisions through Ray-Ban Meta smart glasses, smart devices, and OpenAI’s APIs. Our app offers real-time insights and recommendations to foster sustainable habits, reduce carbon footprints, and promote environmental awareness.

Purpose

EcoSnap empowers users to make environmentally conscious decisions through Ray-Ban Meta smart glasses, smart devices, and OpenAI’s APIs. Our app offers real-time insights and recommendations to foster sustainable habits, reduce carbon footprints, and promote environmental awareness.

Inspiration

Inspired by the need for sustainable lifestyles, EcoSnap simplifies choosing eco-friendly options using AI and real-time data, helping users seamlessly integrate sustainability into daily decisions.

What It Does

Eco-Friendly Shopping Companion

  • Barcode/QR Code Scanning: Identifies products and retrieves sustainability ratings.
  • Sustainability Ratings: Provides real-time environmental impact info and suggests eco-friendly alternatives.
  • Brand Transparency: Offers detailed brand profiles and sustainability-focused user reviews.

Eco-Friendly Food Companion

  • Food Item Analysis: Evaluates environmental impact and offers sustainability ratings.
  • Alternative Suggestions: Recommends eco-friendly food options based on dietary preferences.
  • Educational Insights: Provides info on food miles, seasonal produce, and sustainable recipes.

Eco-Friendly Waste Reduction Guide

  • Location-Based Recycling Guidance: Offers tailored recycling and waste disposal instructions.
  • Waste Reduction Tips: Provides tips and reusable alternatives.
  • Recycling Center Information: Connects users with local recycling centers and initiatives.

Restaurant Companion

  • Sustainable Restaurant Recommendations: Suggests restaurants with sustainable practices.
  • Environmental Impact Scores: Rates menu items and emphasizes plant-based options.
  • Food Miles and Seasonal Produce: Promotes locally-sourced, seasonal dishes.

Sustainability Ratings and Leaderboard

  • Daily Sustainability Score: Calculates scores based on eco-friendly actions.
  • Leaderboards and Challenges: Encourages competition through leaderboards and challenges.

How We Built It

EcoSnap was developed using OpenAI API, Node.js, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Machine Learning.

Challenges We Ran Into

Integrating Meta Smart glasses was challenging due to the lack of an SDK. We created a workaround using a web server and an API, refining our strategy for reliable results.

Accomplishments That We're Proud Of

We're proud of successfully integrating smart glasses and devices, creating a user-friendly experience that empowers people to make eco-conscious decisions. Winning the hackathon and working with such talented individuals was a highlight.

What We Learned

We deepened our understanding of AI integration, smart technology, and sustainable living. The project reinforced the importance of teamwork and adaptability in overcoming unexpected challenges.

What's Next for EcoSnap

Our goal is to expand EcoSnap's features, including more product categories, deeper brand transparency, and enhanced AI capabilities. We plan to collaborate with sustainability organizations to further promote eco-friendly habits.
